ABAC Policy and Policy Statement

Dialog together with the companies within the Dialog Axiata PLC Group recognises and strongly believes that its stakeholders are fundamental to the realisation of its vision. The Group strongly upholds its beliefs and core values of Uncompromising Integrity and Exceptional Performance. It is committed to ensuring zero tolerance of any forms of bribery and corruption in the course of its business.
